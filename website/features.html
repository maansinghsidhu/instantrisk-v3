Zeus Engine

Deep-Learning AI Analysis

Zeus Engine is not a single model — it's an orchestrated team of AI specialists. When you upload documents, Zeus runs specialized modules for each task simultaneously, combining their findings into a unified risk assessment.

  • ✓ OCR Engine — Extracts text from PDFs, images, and scanned documents with 97% accuracy. Handles ACORD forms, handwritten notes, stamps, and multi-column layouts.
  • ✓ Risk Scoring Engine — Analyzes extracted data against 8 risk categories (Property, Casualty, Marine, Aviation, Energy, Specialty, Cyber, Financial Lines) to compute a 0-100 risk score.
  • ✓ Clause Analysis Module — Searches 50,000+ clauses via vector embeddings to identify relevant wordings, missing exclusions, and coverage gaps.
  • ✓ Compliance Engine — Validates submissions against Lloyd's minimum standards, regulatory requirements, and syndicate appetite parameters.
  • ✓ Decision Engine — Synthesizes all module outputs into a GO or NO-GO decision with confidence scoring, detailed rationale, and recommended next steps.

ClaimSense

Benchmark Intelligence

ClaimSense transforms raw loss data into actionable intelligence. Query industry benchmarks, compare peer performance, and validate your pricing assumptions with real market data.

  • ✓ Loss Ratio Benchmarks — Compare any risk's loss ratio against industry averages by territory, class of business, and time period.
  • ✓ Claims Trend Analysis — Spot deteriorating or improving claims trends before they hit the market. Frequency and severity tracking across all classes.
  • ✓ Peer Comparison — See how your portfolio performs against comparable syndicates. Identify over-exposed segments and underpriced business.
  • ✓ Chat Integration — Ask "Compare this risk's loss ratio to industry average" in the assessment chat and get instant benchmark data.

RapidRate

Technical Pricing in Seconds

RapidRate uses machine learning models trained on historical premium and loss data to produce technical rate indications. Factor in territory, construction type, occupancy, loss history, and market cycle position.

  • ✓ ML-Powered Models — Pricing models trained on 10 years of market data, updated quarterly with new loss information.
  • ✓ Market Rate Bands — See where your rate sits relative to the market. Identify over-rated and under-rated segments.
  • ✓ Adequacy Scoring — Automatic adequacy assessment comparing quoted premium against technical rate with confidence intervals.
  • ✓ Scenario Testing — Adjust deductibles, limits, and coverage to see how pricing changes in real-time.
