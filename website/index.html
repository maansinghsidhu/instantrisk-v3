The Zeus Engine

AI That Thinks Like an Underwriter

Zeus Engine is our proprietary multi-layered AI analysis system. It doesn't just read documents — it understands coverage structures, identifies exclusion gaps, cross-references loss histories, and validates premium adequacy against market benchmarks.

  • ✓ Multi-analysis pipeline — Specialized modules for OCR, risk scoring, clause analysis, and compliance checks working in parallel
  • ✓ RAG-powered knowledge — Vector search across 50,000+ insurance clauses, market wordings, and Lloyd's guidelines
  • ✓ Contextual reasoning — Understands relationships between ACORD forms, slip documents, loss runs, and policy schedules
  • ✓ Instant decisions — GO or NO-GO with detailed rationale, confidence scores, and recommended terms

Upload Documents
